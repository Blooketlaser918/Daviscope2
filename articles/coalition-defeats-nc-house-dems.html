Grassroots Coalition Spends $1M+ to Unseat Three NC House Democrats in 2026 Primaries

Published April 3, 2026

State reporting described coordinated campaign activity by advocacy groups in selected primary contests. The outcome demonstrates how organized issue coalitions can influence candidate competitiveness and legislative caucus composition.

Regardless of party alignment, these races are part of a broader pattern in which policy-focused organizations invest heavily in primaries to shape later legislative agendas.

Why this matters locally

Statehouse shifts can affect county priorities in budgets, regulations, and implementation timelines.
