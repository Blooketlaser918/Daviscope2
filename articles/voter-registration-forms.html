NC Stopped Printing Free Bulk Voter Registration Forms, Raising Access Concerns for Local Drives

Published March 20, 2026

Reports indicated changes to free bulk voter registration form distribution. For local civic groups, printing costs and distribution logistics can influence how quickly outreach efforts scale in advance of election deadlines.

Official election sources remain essential for current registration instructions and eligibility requirements.

Why this matters locally

Access to registration materials can affect participation, especially for community-led drives in smaller jurisdictions.
