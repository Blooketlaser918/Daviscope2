About Us

DaviScope is a student-built civic information website focused on helping Davidson County residents stay informed.

Our Purpose

DaviScope was created to assist the public with civics-related information in a clear and organized way. For our project, we chose to focus on informing the Davidson County public on recent political events, local and state politicians, and elections. Our goal is to support the welfare of the Davidson County community by making trusted civic information easier to find and follow.

Who Created DaviScope?

DaviScope was assembled by Zach, Joseph, and Deacon. We are high school students participating in a project that challenged us to build a website that serves the public through civic awareness and education.

Our Mission

We built this website primarily so people in Davidson County can stay informed. By highlighting relevant political developments and election resources, we aim to make civic participation easier and more accessible for everyone in our community.
